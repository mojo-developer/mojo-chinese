Privacy Policy for Mojo Chinese

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Data Collection

Mojo Chinese respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The app processes your voice and image data locally on your device or via secure, encrypted native API connections (Apple/Third-party Speech & Translation frameworks) solely for the purpose of providing translation services. We do not store your photos or voice recordings on any of our servers.

2. Subscriptions & Analytics

We use Apple's native StoreKit for processing in-app purchases. All payment information is securely handled by Apple, and we do not have access to your credit card details. We may access anonymous crash logs provided by Apple to improve app stability.
